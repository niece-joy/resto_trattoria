About us

I'm so excited to announce the opening of TRATTORIA, Cagayan newest restaurant! We are a family-owned and operated Italian restaurant serving up delicious dishes inspired by authentic, traditional recipes. We believe that food should be enjoyed with friends and family and that's why we strive to make our restaurant a warm and inviting place where people can come together and share good times.

Our menu features a variety of Italian favorites, from lasagna and pasta to pizza and desserts. We also offer a selection of specialty dishes that incorporate fresh, local ingredients and flavors, so there's always something new to try. We also have a full bar with an extensive wine list, so you can enjoy a glass of wine with your meal.

Come and join us for a unique dining experience that you won't find anywhere else. We look forward to seeing you soon!

OUR VISION

Our vision is to create an authentic Italian dining experience that transports our guests to the heart of Italy. We believe that food is not just about sustenance, but about creating memories and bringing people together. We want to be known as the go-to destination for Italian food in the area, with a menu that showcases the best of traditional and modern Italian cuisine. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a night out with friends and family, we want our trattoria to be a place where you can make lasting memories and savor the flavors of Italy.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide our customers with high-quality products that exceed their expectations. We are committed to creating a positive impact on our community and the environment through our business practices. We strive to be a leader in our industry by constantly innovating and improving our products. Our goal is to build long-lasting relationships with our clients by providing exceptional customer service. We believe in empowering our employees to reach their full potential and creating a positive work culture. At trattoria, we are dedicated to making a difference in the world and being a company that our customers and employees can be proud of.
